How Staycation Helps The Environment

Transportation is a big culprit in the human caused climate change we are experiencing.
We can all do our part to change the tide, even the smallest action can have a big impact, starting with our vacations.
Staycation offers the possibility of having a holiday in a local area, exploring and enjoying the local towns and natural landscapes,
without using mass transportations like airplanes, thus having a smaller impact on the climate.
