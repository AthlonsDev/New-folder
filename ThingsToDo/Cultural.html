Nuraghi

Giants Tombs

Osono Area

Neolothic complexes, probably related to the cult of he dead, they take the name from their size and ancient purpose.
It is a protected National Park, so tourists are only allowed on guided tours

Garibaldi's Musuem

Caprera Island

Caprera was the last home to the greatest hero in Italian hisory, who unified the country: Giuseppe Garibaldi.
Garibaldi's house has been turned into a museum and visits can be book here.

Murales

San Sperate, Orgosolo and more

Amazing works of street art, these artworks shaped these little towns, making them free open air art galleries.
Find accomodations in these characteristic towns.
