Budelli Beach

Island

Little island off the coast of Sardinia, famous for its pink sand.
It is a protected National Park, so tourists are only allowed on guided tours

Costa Smeralda

Beach

One of the most popular locations for foreign tourists, often overlooked by the locals because of its luxuriuos resorts.
But it can offer wonderful experiences for local tourists and more affordable accomodation.

Beach

Southern Area

Poetto Beach

Cagliari

Capital's local beach, very popular for its citizens, it's located in the centre of the city.
One of the biggest beaches in Italy, find accomodation in the city, from where it's most convenient to experience this location.

Calasetta Beach

Stant'Antioco

Exotic location in the south east, on the island of Sant'Antioco.
With many beaches and a vibrant town, it's a great summer holiday place.

Porto Giunco

Villasimius

Located in the South West, Villasimius is a vibrant little town full of natural landscape.
With many beaches and lovely hotels and BnBs, it's a great summer holiday place.
